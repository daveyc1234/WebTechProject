About Me

My name is Dave Crouch. I am from Jacksonville, Florida and come from a big family of five children. I have two yellow labs named Jones and Drew. Many of my passions and hobbies revolve around sports as they bring out the ultimate competitive spirit in me.

Last year, I transfered from Clemson University to play basketball in college. I was recruited in high school, and when my little brother was getting recruited, the gave me the option to transfer from Clemson and come here to play with him. I am a junior at Babson College and plan to graduate with a concentration in Finance. After school, I plan to work corporate finance for Boston Scientific. I had an internship there this summer and am very excited to continue working there.

My Hobbies

Being interested in a lot of different hobbies is something that I believe is important to have because you can find many friends with many different kinds of personalities. Most of my hobbies take place outdoors on the weekends when I can escape from my schoolwork. I love to go fishing, hunting, swimming, kayaking, surfing, and hiking. Despite how much fun I have with those activities, if I could chose one hobby to do for the rest of my life, it would be golf.
